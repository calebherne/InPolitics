# Micah Beckwith - Indiana Lieutenant Governor  

## Office
[Lieutenant Governor](../../Offices/State/Lieutenant%20Governor.md)

## Basic Information
- **Name:** Micah Adam Beckwith
- **Party:** Republican
- **Assumed Office:** January 13, 2025
- **Running Mate:** Mike Braun

## Background
- **Born:** August 1982 (Age 43) in Hillsdale, Michigan
- **Nationality:** American
- **Generations in U.S.:** Information not readily available
- **Religion:** Christian (Pastor at Life Church, Noblesville) - Self-described "Christian nationalist" ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith))
- **Education:**  - Huntington University (BA in Business Economics)
- **Occupation:** Pastor at Life Church, Noblesville, Indiana
- **Family:** Wife Susan (Miss Indiana 2005), 2 children
- **Current Residence:** Noblesville, Indiana

## Political Career Timeline
- **2020:** Ran for U.S. House (IN-5th District), finished 3rd in Republican primary with 12.7%
- **2022-2024:** Hamilton East Public Library Board of Trustees member (controversial)
- **2024:** Won Republican nomination for Lt. Governor, defeating Mike Braun's preferred choice Julie McGuire
- **2025-Present:** Lieutenant Governor of Indiana

## Immigration Stance
**Mixed/Controversial position:**
- **2024:** Described Haitian resettlement in Logansport as burden on public services, blamed Biden for extending temporary protected status, said people "should not be here" ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))
- **2025:** Posted about meeting with Haitian pastors, praised their work ethic, encouraged legal immigration and assimilation, then deleted post after conservative backlash ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))
- Claims he hasn't "flip-flopped," emphasizes any immigration must be legal with English learning and assimilation

## What He's Known For

### Controversial Statements & Actions
- **"Jezebel Spirit" Comment:** Called Democratic ticket's support of abortion/gender-affirming care "Jezebel spirit" and "demonic" ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))
- **Three-Fifths Compromise (2025):** Defended the Three-Fifths Compromise as "a great move" that "helped root out slavery" - condemned by religious leaders as "historical revisionism" ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))
- **IU Student Newspaper (2024):** Threatened to defund Indiana Daily Student for "WOKE propaganda," later clarified would protect free speech if stories "fair" ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))
- **Senate Reprimand (2025):** Received formal written reprimand from Senate President for using AI-enabled smart glasses during sessions and posting on X during active votes ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))

### Library Book Controversy
- Led effort to remove nearly 2,000 titles from Hamilton East Public Library young adult section
- Policy barred materials with "depictions of sex, violence and repeated profanity"
- Attracted national attention, criticism from author John Green
- Policy suspended then repealed after conservative board members resigned
- Beckwith resigned from board in 2024 to run for Lt. Governor ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith))

### Grand Jury Investigation (2025)
- October 2025: Marion County grand jury investigating office for alleged ghost employment and distribution of intimate images
- Allegation involves deepfake AI video of Republican lawmaker's wife shown in office
- No charges filed, Beckwith denies knowledge ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))

## Political Positions
- **Abortion:** Strongly opposes, would charge rapist with homicide if victim aborted pregnancy ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Abortion))
- **Capital Punishment:** Supports death penalty ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Capital_punishment))
- **Medical Cannabis:** Supports legalization for medical use in Indiana ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Drug_policy_reform))
- **LGBT Rights:** Opposed library materials with LGBT content, fought against protections

## Relationship with Governor Braun
- **Not Braun's Choice:** Braun preferred Julie McGuire for Lt. Gov, but GOP delegates chose Beckwith at state convention
- **Public Disagreements:** Braun has publicly criticized Beckwith's Three-Fifths Compromise comments, saying he "wouldn't have used that characterization" ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Micah_Beckwith#Lieutenant_Governor_of_Indiana))
