## Office Title Change
**Important**: On July 1, 2023, Governor Holcomb signed legislation authorizing the State Auditor to [do business as "the State Comptroller"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tera_Klutz#Career). The formal constitutional title remains "State Auditor" - any permanent change would require a constitutional amendment. Under Nieshalla, the office now uses "State Comptroller" branding.

## What She's Known For

### As State Comptroller (2023-present):
- **National debt advocacy**: Led [national call to confront America's debt crisis](https://www.in.gov/comptroller/files/Indiana-State-Comptroller-Elise-Nieshalla-Leads-National-Call-to-Confront-Americas-Debt-Crisis.pdf) (November 2025)

- **Federal debt resolution**: Indiana's resolution on federal debt [gained national traction](https://www.in.gov/comptroller/files/Indianas-Resolution-on-Federal-Debt-Gains-National-Traction.pdf) (August 2025)

- **U.S. Capitol forum**: Spoke at [National Debt Crisis Forum at the U.S. Capitol](https://www.in.gov/comptroller/files/State-Comptroller-Nieshalla-Speaks-at-National-Debt-Crisis-Forum-at-the-U.S.-Capitol.pdf) (September 2025)

- **Financial excellence**: Indiana [earned top national award for financial reporting](https://www.in.gov/comptroller/files/State-Comptroller-Indiana-Earns-Top-National-Award-for-Financial-Reporting.pdf) under her leadership (September 2025)

- **Fiscal discipline**: Reported [$676 million surplus](https://www.in.gov/comptroller/files/2025-State-of-Indianas-Financial-Report_RELEASE.pdf), low debt, and AAA credit rating (February 2026)

- **Hoosier START Award**: Announced award recognizing state employees (August 2025)

### Office Modernization:
- Actively promoting "State Comptroller" title to better reflect modern duties
- Publishing monthly "[Connecting with the Comptroller](https://www.in.gov/comptroller/files/December-Connecting-with-the-Comptroller-Newsletter.pdf)" newsletter

- Chief financial officer for the state
- Oversees state finances and accounting
- Manages Hoosier START (state employee retirement program)
- Chairs State Board of Finance
- Publishes Annual Comprehensive Financial Reports
- Provides resources to local governments

- Monthly Comptroller's Roundtable meetings

## Previous Auditor: Tera Klutz
Nieshalla's predecessor, [Tera Klutz](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tera_Klutz), served 2017-2023:
- First Certified Public Accountant to be Indiana Auditor
- Emphasized transparency and technology
- Resigned November 30, 2023 for private sector position
- Succeeded [Suzanne Crouch](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suzanne_Crouch) (who became Lieutenant Governor)
