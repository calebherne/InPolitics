# State Senator - J.D. Ford (District 29)

**Office**: [State Senate](../../Offices/State/State%20Senate.md)  
**District**: 29 (includes parts of Hamilton County)  
**Party**: Democrat  
**Term**: 4 years

## Background

J.D. Ford represents Indiana State Senate District 29, which includes portions of Hamilton County. He is one of the few Democrats representing parts of Hamilton County in the state legislature.
