# State Representative - Victoria Garcia Wilburn (District 32)

**Office**: [State House of Representatives](../../Offices/State/State%20House%20of%20Representatives.md)  
**District**: 32 (includes parts of Hamilton County)  
**Party**: Democrat  
**Term**: 2 years

## Background

Victoria Garcia Wilburn represents Indiana House District 32, which includes portions of Hamilton County. She is one of the few Democrats representing parts of Hamilton County in the state legislature.
