# State Treasurer - Daniel Elliott

## Basic Information
- **Name**: Daniel Elliott  
- **Party**: Republican
- **Office**: Treasurer of Indiana
- **Assumed Office**: January 9, 2023
- **Age**: Information not publicly available (born in Toledo, Ohio)

## Background
- **Birthplace**: Toledo, Ohio
- **Nationality**: American
- **Generations in US**: Information not publicly available
- **Religion**: [Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Church_of_Jesus_Christ_of_Latter-day_Saints) (LDS/Mormon) - **First Latter-day Saint to hold statewide elected office in Indiana**
- **Missionary Service**: Served 2-year LDS mission in [Argentina](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Argentina)
- **Education**:
  - [Utah Valley University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Utah_Valley_University) (BS in Business and Computer Science)
- **Family**: Married to Laura Elliott; 4 children
- **Residence**: [Green Township](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_Township,_Morgan_County,_Indiana), Morgan County, Indiana (rural area)
- **Career**: Software development business owner based in Martinsville, Indiana

## Political Career Timeline
- **2012**: Ran for Indiana House of Representatives, lost Republican primary to Peggy Mayfield
- **2013**: Elected Chair of Morgan County Republican Party
- **2016**: Elected to Morgan County Council
- **President**: Morgan County Redevelopment Commission (many years)
- **Member**: Morgan County Economic Development Corporation
- **2022**: Elected Indiana State Treasurer (defeated Democrat Jessica McClellan in general election)
- **2023-present**: State Treasurer

## What He's Known For

### Conservative Social Positions:
- **2018 Republican Platform**: Led successful [movement to "endorse marriage between a man and a woman as the ideal basis for strong families"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniel_Elliott_(Indiana_politician)#Early_life_and_career) in Indiana Republican Party platform.

### Professional Background:
- Software development and technology entrepreneur
- Extensive local government experience in Morgan County
- Focus on economic development and redevelopment

### As State Treasurer:
- Serves as **Chief Investment Officer** for all 6.7 million Hoosiers
- Oversees:
  - [TrustINdiana](https://www.trustindiana.in.gov/) - Local Government Investment Pool
  - [INvestABLE Indiana](https://www.in.gov/tos/able) - Tax-advantaged savings for individuals with disabilities
  - [Indiana529](https://www.myindiana529.com/) - College savings plans
- Emphasizes fiscal responsibility: "My charge is to wisely invest the state's funds in order to minimize the risk and maximize the interest earned."

### Historic Significance:
- First member of the [Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Church_of_Jesus_Christ_of_Latter-day_Saints) (LDS Church) to hold statewide elected office in Indiana

### Notable:
- Ran on "Strong Families" platform emphasizing traditional marriage
- Background in technology sector brings modern approach to state finances
- Experience at county level in redevelopment and economic development
