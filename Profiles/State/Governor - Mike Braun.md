# Mike Braun - Indiana Governor

## Office
[Governor](../../Offices/State/Governor.md)

## Basic Information
- **Name:** Michael Kent Braun
- **Party:** Republican
- **Assumed Office:** January 13, 2025
- **Previous Position:** U.S. Senator from Indiana (2019-2025)

## Background
- **Born:** March 24, 1954 (Age 71) in Jasper, Indiana
- **Nationality:** American
- **Generations in U.S.:** Information not readily available, but family from Dubois County, Indiana (German-descended area)
- **Religion:** Catholic ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun))
- **Education:** 
  - Wabash College (BA in Economics, summa cum laude)
  - Harvard Business School (MBA)
- **Occupation:** Businessman - Meyer Distributing (President/CEO)
- **Family:** Wife Maureen, 4 children

## Political Career Timeline
- **2004-2014:** Jasper School Board member
- **2014-2017:** Indiana State Representative (63rd District)
- **2019-2025:** U.S. Senator from Indiana
- **2025-Present:** Governor of Indiana

## Immigration Stance
**Hardline restrictionist position:**
- "Building the wall must be the first step to any solution" on illegal immigration ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Immigration))
- Opposes pathway to citizenship for DREAMers (undocumented immigrants who came as minors) ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Immigration))
- Supports border wall construction
- Previously was Democrat until 2012, switched to Republican

## What He's Known For
- **Business Background:** Built Meyer Distributing from 15 employees to 300+ employees
- **Trump Ally:** Voted with Trump 90.9% of the time during Senate tenure ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Donald_Trump))
- **Election 2020 Controversy:** Initially pledged to object to 2020 election results, reversed after Jan 6 Capitol attack ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Effort_to_overturn_2020_presidential_election_result))
- **Interracial Marriage Controversy (2022):** Said Loving v. Virginia should be left to states, later walked back comments ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Interracial_marriage))
- **Climate Advocacy:** Calls himself a "conservationist," supports carbon pricing, cited Greta Thunberg as "inspiration" ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Environmentalism))
- **Property Tax Focus:** Ran on platform of addressing rising property taxes
- **Party Switch:** Was registered Democrat until 2012, voted in Democratic primaries historically in Dubois County

## Recent Controversies
- **IU Trustees (2025):** Moved to remove alumni-elected trustees and replace with gubernatorial appointees ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Higher_education_appointments))
- **Dolly Parton Library (2025):** Declined to support state match for Imagination Library program ([source](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Braun#Early_childhood_learning))
