## Basic Info
- **Full Name**: Kristina Wheeler

## Background
Kristina Wheeler serves as a member of the Carmel Clay Schools Board of Trustees, elected to an at-large seat representing the entire Carmel Clay school district.

## Role on Board
As an at-large board member:
- Votes on all district policies and budgets
- Hires and evaluates the superintendent
- Sets district goals and strategic direction
- Represents all district constituents

## Carmel Clay Schools
Carmel Clay Schools is one of Indiana's largest and highest-performing school districts, serving Carmel and parts of Clay Township.
