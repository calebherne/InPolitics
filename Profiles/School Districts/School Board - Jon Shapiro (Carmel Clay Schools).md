## Basic Info
- **Full Name**: Jon Shapiro
- **Position**: President, Carmel Clay Schools Board of Trustees
- **Seat Type**: At-Large (elected countywide within district)
- **Term**: Four years

## Background
Jon Shapiro serves as President of the Carmel Clay Schools Board of Trustees, elected to an at-large seat representing the entire Carmel Clay school district.

## Role on Board
As an at-large board member and president:
- Presides over board meetings
- Sets board meeting agendas (with superintendent)
- Serves as spokesperson for the board
- Votes on all district policies and budgets
- Hires and evaluates the superintendent
- Sets district goals and strategic direction

## Carmel Clay Schools
Carmel Clay Schools is one of Indiana's largest and highest-performing school districts, serving Carmel and parts of Clay Township.
