# City Mayor - Scott Fadness (Fishers)

**Office**: [City Mayor](../../Offices/City/City%20Mayor.md)

## Basic Info
- **Full Name**: Scott Fadness

- **City**: Fishers, Indiana
- **Email**: mayorfadness@fishersin.gov
- **Family**: Married to Aunna, three sons (Lincoln, Hudson, Jackson)

## Background
Scott Fadness became Fishers' first Mayor on January 1, 2015, when the town transitioned to city status. He previously served as Fishers' Town Manager and as an adjunct professor at the Indiana University-Purdue University Indianapolis School of Public Affairs. He holds a Master's degree in Public Affairs from Indiana University.

## Career as Mayor
Under Scott's leadership:
- **2017**: Fishers named "Best Place to Live in America" by Money Magazine
- **2016**: Named "Community of the Year" by the Indiana Chamber of Commerce
- Created more than 4,900 new jobs and $500 million in new private investment in his first term
- Revitalized downtown Fishers (Nickel Plate District)
- Attracted major employers and amenities including IKEA and TopGolf
- Created Launch Fishers entrepreneurial hub
- Developed Agripark (nation's largest urban farm)
- Built Nickel Plate District Amphitheater
- Created The Yard at Fishers District culinary destination
- Developed Geist Waterfront Park

## What He's Known For
- Transforming Fishers from a bedroom community into a vibrant city
- Focus on entrepreneurship and innovation
- Smart city initiatives and technology adoption
- Attracting corporate headquarters to Fishers
- Building quality-of-life amenities
