# City Mayor - Chris Jensen (Noblesville)

**Office**: [City Mayor](../../Offices/City/City%20Mayor.md)  
**City**: Noblesville  
**Party**: Republican  
**Term**: Elected 2019, currently serving

## Basic Info
- **Full Name**: Chris Jensen
- **Position**: 29th Mayor of Noblesville, Indiana
- **City**: Noblesville (county seat of Hamilton County)
- **Family**: Married to Julie Jensen (instructional coach at Noblesville Schools), four children (CJ, Vivian, Hank, Emmy Kate)
- **Education**: Butler University graduate
- **Church**: White River Christian Church in Noblesville

## Background
Chris Jensen is a lifelong Noblesville resident ("Miller") who has served as the 29th Mayor since being elected in 2019. Prior to serving as Mayor, Jensen served on the Noblesville Common Council and worked in business development for a civil engineering firm where he assisted cities and counties across Indiana with infrastructure development.

He previously served in the administration of former Indiana Governor Mitch Daniels, working as special assistant to Lieutenant Governor Becky Skillman and director of Intergovernmental Affairs.

## Accomplishments as Mayor
- Attracted more than $1 billion in new private investment
- Led transformational infrastructure development including Pleasant Street project
- Launched "Mental Health Mondays" online series addressing critical community issues
- Created the "Noble Act" program revolutionizing public safety teamwork
- Successfully led city through COVID-19 pandemic challenges

## Policy Pillars
1. **Raising the Quality of Life and Culture** - Enhancing downtown and making Noblesville a regional destination
2. **Making Infrastructure Connections** - Pleasant Street project, SR-37 improvements, potential I-69 interchange
3. **Unmatched Public Safety** - Supporting first responders and exploring new public safety facility
4. **Taking Care of Taxpayer Dollars** - Fiscal responsibility while improving services
