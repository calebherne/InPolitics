## Background
Scott Willis has served as the Mayor of Westfield, Indiana since 2024. As a decorated U.S. Marine Colonel, successful businessman, and longtime Westfield resident, Scott brings military leadership experience and business acumen to the Mayor's Office.

## Vision and Priorities
From day one as Mayor, Scott has been committed to:
- Diversifying Westfield's tax base
- Identifying new businesses and jobs
- Fiscal responsibility
- Strategically managing the city's growth
- Community-focused governance
