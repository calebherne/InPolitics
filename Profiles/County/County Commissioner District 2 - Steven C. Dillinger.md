## Background
Steven C. Dillinger serves as the Hamilton County Commissioner for District 2, representing the central portions of Hamilton County.

## Role as Commissioner
As a County Commissioner, Dillinger:
- Serves on the three-member executive body of Hamilton County
- Participates in county budget decisions
- Oversees county departments and operations
- Makes decisions on infrastructure and development projects
- Works with County Council on fiscal matters

## District 2
