## Background
The Hamilton County Circuit Court Clerk maintains court records and manages administrative operations for Hamilton County's courts.

## Role as Circuit Court Clerk
As Circuit Court Clerk, responsibilities include:
- Maintaining all court case files and records
- Processing court filings and documents
- Managing jury selection process
- Collecting court fees and fines
- Issuing marriage licenses
- Recording court proceedings
- Managing court schedules
- Providing public access to court records
- Overseeing clerk's office staff
