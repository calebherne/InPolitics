- **Position**: Hamilton County Sheriff

## Background
The Hamilton County Sheriff serves as the chief law enforcement officer for Hamilton County, overseeing the Sheriff's Office operations including the jail, patrol, investigations, and court security.

## Role as Sheriff
As County Sheriff, responsibilities include:
- Leading the Hamilton County Sheriff's Office
- Managing the Hamilton County Jail
- Providing court security and bailiff services
- Patrol and law enforcement in unincorporated areas
- Serving legal papers and warrants
- Managing civil process (foreclosures, evictions)
- Emergency management coordination

## Sheriff's Office
The Hamilton County Sheriff's Office is responsible for law enforcement throughout Hamilton County, particularly in unincorporated areas and providing support to municipal police departments.
