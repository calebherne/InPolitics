## Background
The Hamilton County Treasurer is responsible for receiving, managing, and disbursing county funds, particularly property tax collections.

## Role as Treasurer
As County Treasurer, responsibilities include:
- Collecting property taxes
- Managing county investment portfolio
- Disbursing tax revenue to taxing units (schools, cities, townships)
- Maintaining custody of county funds
- Processing tax payments and settlements
- Managing delinquent tax collections
- Investing idle county funds
- Reporting on county finances
