## Background
The Hamilton County Coroner is responsible for investigating deaths, determining causes of death, and maintaining death records for Hamilton County.

## Role as Coroner
As County Coroner, responsibilities include:
- Investigating all unnatural, suspicious, or unexpected deaths
- Determining cause and manner of death
- Ordering autopsies when necessary
- Issuing death certificates
- Testifying in court proceedings
- Managing the county morgue
- Coordinating with law enforcement on death investigations
