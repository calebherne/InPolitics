## Background
The Hamilton County Surveyor is responsible for maintaining accurate property boundary information and conducting land surveys throughout the county.

## Role as Surveyor
As County Surveyor, responsibilities include:
- Maintaining county survey records and monuments
- Conducting official property boundary surveys
- Managing county drainage systems
- Establishing and preserving property corners
- Resolving boundary disputes
- Reviewing subdivision plats
- Maintaining Geographic Information System (GIS) data
- Overseeing county drainage board
