# County Council At-Large - Sue Maki

**Office**: [County Council](../../Offices/County/County%20Council.md)

## Basic Info
- **Full Name**: Sue Maki
- **Position**: Hamilton County Council Member, At-Large
- **County**: Hamilton County, Indiana

## Background
Sue Maki serves as an at-large member of the Hamilton County Council, elected countywide rather than from a specific district. She is one of three at-large members on the seven-member council.

## Role on Council
As a County Council member, Maki:
- Serves on the legislative and fiscal body of Hamilton County
- Represents the entire county (elected countywide)
- Votes on county budget and appropriations
- Reviews and approves county spending
- Sets tax rates and levies
- Approves county employee salaries
- Works with County Commissioners on fiscal policy

## At-Large Position
At-large council members are elected by all Hamilton County voters, rather than from a specific district, providing a countywide perspective.
