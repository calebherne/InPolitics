## Background
Christine Altman serves as the Hamilton County Commissioner for District 1, representing the northern and western portions of Hamilton County.

## Role as Commissioner
As a County Commissioner, Altman:
- Serves on the three-member executive body of Hamilton County
- Participates in county budget decisions
- Oversees county departments and operations
- Makes decisions on infrastructure and development projects
- Works with County Council on fiscal matters

## District 1
District 1 covers portions of Hamilton County including areas in the northern and western sections of the county.
