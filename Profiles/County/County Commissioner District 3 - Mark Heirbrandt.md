## Background
Mark Heirbrandt serves as the Hamilton County Commissioner for District 3, representing the southern and eastern portions of Hamilton County.

## Role as Commissioner
As a County Commissioner, Heirbrandt:
- Serves on the three-member executive body of Hamilton County
- Participates in county budget decisions
- Oversees county departments and operations
- Makes decisions on infrastructure and development projects
- Works with County Council on fiscal matters

## District 3
District 3 covers portions of Hamilton County including areas in the southern and eastern sections of the county.
