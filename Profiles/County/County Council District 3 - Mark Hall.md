## Background
Mark Hall serves as a member of the Hamilton County Council representing District 3, part of the seven-member legislative body of Hamilton County.

## Role on Council
As a County Council member, Hall:
- Serves on the legislative and fiscal body of Hamilton County
- Votes on county budget and appropriations
- Reviews and approves county spending
- Sets tax rates and levies
- Approves county employee salaries
- Works with County Commissioners on fiscal policy

## District 3
District 3 is one of four single-member districts on the County Council.
