## Background
The Hamilton County Recorder maintains the official records of property ownership and transfers in Hamilton County.

## Role as Recorder
As County Recorder, responsibilities include:
- Recording deeds, mortgages, and property transfers
- Maintaining land ownership records
- Recording liens and releases
- Preserving historical property documents
- Providing public access to recorded documents
- Indexing and filing real estate documents
- Managing digital records system
- Certifying copies of recorded documents
