## Background
The Hamilton County Auditor serves as the chief fiscal officer of the county, managing financial records, payroll, and budgets.

## Role as Auditor
As County Auditor, responsibilities include:
- Maintaining county financial records
- Processing county payroll
- Managing county budget documentation
- Issuing tax bills and property tax statements
- Serving as secretary to the County Commissioners and County Council
- Maintaining records of county board meetings
- Processing accounts payable
- Managing county investments
