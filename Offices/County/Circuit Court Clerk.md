# Circuit Court Clerk

## Requirements
- Must declare party affiliation
- Resident of Hamilton County

## Election Process
- Elected county-wide
- Partisan election

## Term
- **Length:** 4 years
- **Term Limits:** None
- **Next Election:** November 2026

## Roles
- **Full-time position** - Chief administrative officer for county courts
- Clerk of [County Circuit Court](County%20Circuit%20Court.md)
- Maintains all court case files and records
- Files and processes court documents
- Collects court fees and fines
- Issues marriage licenses
- Processes passport applications
- Manages jury selection and coordination
- Maintains case management system
- Provides public access to court records
- **Time Commitment:** Full-time, 40-50 hours/week
- **Meetings:** Daily with court staff; Judges coordination; Public service counter; Record requests

## Powers
**Constitutional Powers:**
- **Official court records** keeper
- **Filing authority** - Accepts/processes court documents
- **Fee collection** for court services
- **Marriage licenses** issuance
- **Jury administration** authority
- **Passport services** authorization
- **Public access** custodian for court records

**Procedural Powers:**
- Controls court filing procedures and deadlines
- Can expedite or delay document processing
- Fee schedules within statutory limits
- Access to court records (public vs. sealed)
- Technology choices affect court efficiency
- Jury selection process affects trials
- Platform to advocate for court access/efficiency
- Works closely with judges (cooperation matters)
- Court calendar management affects case timing
- Marriage license procedures can be strict or accommodating
