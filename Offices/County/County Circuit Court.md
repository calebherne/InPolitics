# Hamilton County Circuit Court

## Requirements
- Licensed attorney
- Admitted to practice law in Indiana
- Resident of circuit

## Selection Process
- Elected by county voters
- Partisan election
- Each county has at least one circuit court (constitutional requirement)

## Term
- **Length:** 6 years
- **Term Limits:** None
- **Mandatory Retirement:** Age 75

## Roles
- **Full-time position** - Trial court judge with general jurisdiction
- Constitutional court (established by Indiana Constitution)
- Presides over civil and criminal trials
- Hears family law matters (divorce, custody, etc.)
- Handles criminal prosecutions
- Rules on motions and pretrial matters
- Sentences convicted defendants
- Issues protective orders
- Manages court docket and calendar
- Conducts hearings and settlement conferences
- Original jurisdiction over most legal matters in county
- **Time Commitment:** Full-time, 50-60+ hours/week
- **Meetings:** Daily court sessions; Weekly docket review; Judicial conferences; Sentencing hearings; Trial proceedings

## Powers
**Constitutional Powers:**
- **General jurisdiction** - Can hear any case type
- **Judicial authority** over cases assigned
- **Sentencing power** within statutory limits
- **Contempt power** - Can jail for contempt of court
- **Order issuance** - Restraining orders, protective orders
- **Evidence rulings** - Determines what jury sees
- **Bond determination** for criminal defendants
- **Jury instructions** - Guides jury deliberations

**Procedural Powers:**
- Sentencing discretion within ranges (can be lenient or harsh)
- Can expedite or delay cases through docket management
- Evidentiary rulings can determine case outcomes
- Plea bargain approval affects criminal justice
- Bond decisions determine pretrial detention
- Can refer cases for diversion programs
- Platform to advocate for judicial reforms
- Appointment of special masters, receivers, guardians
- Can pressure settlements through rulings
- Media attention on high-profile cases
- Pretrial release decisions affect defendants' lives
