# Hamilton County Recorder

## Requirements
- Must declare party affiliation
- Resident of Hamilton County

## Election Process
- Elected county-wide
- Partisan election

## Term
- **Length:** 4 years
- **Term Limits:** None
- **Next Election:** November 2026

## Roles
- **Full-time position** - Official record keeper for property transactions
- Records and maintains public land records
- Records deeds and mortgages
- Maintains property transfer records
- Records liens and judgments
- Maintains plat maps
- Provides certified copies of recorded documents
- Public records custodian for land transactions
- Collects recording fees
- Modernizes and digitizes records
- **Time Commitment:** Full-time, 40-50 hours/week
- **Meetings:** Daily with staff; Technology/digitization planning; Public records requests; Coordination with title companies and attorneys

## Powers
**Constitutional Powers:**
- **Official record keeper** for property documents
- **Recording authority** - Must record proper documents
- **Certification power** - Issues official copies
- **Fee collection** for recording services
- **Preservation responsibility** for historical records
- **Public access** custodian for land records

**Procedural Powers:**
- Can refuse improper documents (gatekeeping)
- Indexing and search functionality affects title searches
- Digitization priorities affect accessibility
- Fee schedules within statutory limits
- Can modernize or maintain traditional processes
- Platform to advocate for property records access
- Controls turnaround time for recordings
- Technology choices affect efficiency and vendors
- Historical records preservation choices
