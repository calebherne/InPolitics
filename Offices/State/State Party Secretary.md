# State Party Secretary (Republican Party of Indiana)

## Overview
The State Party Secretary is an officer of the Republican Party of Indiana responsible for maintaining party records, documenting meetings, and ensuring proper administrative procedures.

## Requirements
- Must be elected by the State Committee
- Must be a registered Republican voter in Indiana
- No specific residency, age, or citizenship requirements beyond being a voter

## How Elected
- Elected by the State Committee
- Typically elected at the State Convention or State Committee meeting
- Elections held every two years (even-numbered years)
- Elected by majority vote of State Committee members present

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Record Keeping**: Maintain official records of State Committee meetings and proceedings
- **Meeting Minutes**: Record and distribute minutes of all State Committee meetings
- **Document Management**: Manage party documents, bylaws, and official correspondence
- **Notice Requirements**: Ensure proper notice is given for meetings per party rules
- **Credential Verification**: May assist in verifying credentials of delegates and committee members
- **Archival Duties**: Maintain historical records of party decisions and actions

**Time Commitment**: Part-time position
- Attend all State Committee meetings
- Prepare and distribute meeting materials
- Maintain ongoing correspondence and record-keeping
- Coordinate with party staff on administrative matters

## Powers and Limitations
### Powers:
- Custody of official party records and seal (if applicable)
- Certify official party documents and actions
- Vote on State Committee matters
- Access to confidential party information as needed for duties

### Limitations:
- Administrative role with limited policy-making authority
- Subject to direction of State Party Chair and State Committee
- Cannot unilaterally alter official records without proper authorization
- Decisions on party matters require State Committee vote

## Term Length
- 2-year term
- No term limits
- Can be re-elected indefinitely
