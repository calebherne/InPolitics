# State Party Vice Chair (Republican Party of Indiana)

## Overview
The State Party Vice Chair is the second-highest-ranking officer of the Republican Party of Indiana, assisting the State Party Chair and serving in their absence.

## Requirements
- Must be elected by the State Committee
- Must be a registered Republican voter in Indiana
- No specific residency, age, or citizenship requirements beyond being a voter

## How Elected
- Elected by the State Committee
- Typically elected at the same time as the State Party Chair
- Elections held every two years (even-numbered years)
- Elected by majority vote of State Committee members present

## Roles and Responsibilities
- **Assist Party Chair**: Support the State Party Chair in all party operations
- **Substitute Leadership**: Preside over meetings in the Chair's absence
- **Regional Coordination**: Often responsible for coordinating with specific regions or districts
- **Committee Work**: Lead or participate in various party committees
- **Fundraising Support**: Assist with fundraising efforts
- **Public Representation**: Represent the party at events and in media when needed

**Time Commitment**: Part-time to full-time (varies by party needs)
- Attend State Committee meetings and party events
- Coordinate with county parties and district chairs
- Fill in for Chair when needed
- May have specific portfolio responsibilities

## Powers and Limitations
### Powers:
- Exercise powers of the Chair when Chair is absent or unable to serve
- Vote on State Committee matters
- Represent party at events and meetings
- Participate in party leadership decisions

### Limitations:
- Authority generally subordinate to the State Party Chair
- Cannot override decisions of the Chair or State Committee
- Powers when substituting for Chair are typically temporary
- Major decisions require consultation with Chair and/or State Committee

## Term Length
- 2-year term
- No term limits
- Can be re-elected indefinitely
- Succession: Typically becomes acting Chair if Chair position becomes vacant until a new election
