# Hamilton Heights School Corporation

## Overview
School district serving northern Hamilton County.

## Board Structure
- **Board Members:** Elected members (typically 5-7)
- **Superintendent:** Appointed by board
- **Nonpartisan elections**

## Geographic Area
- Northern Hamilton County
- Towns of Arcadia and Atlanta
- Parts of Adams, Jackson, and White River Townships

## Board Member Information
- **Term:** 4 years
- **Elections:** November of even-numbered years
- **Next Election:** November 2026
- Elected by district or at-large (varies)

## District Details
- More rural character than other Hamilton County districts
- Serves multiple small communities
