# Hamilton Southeastern Schools

## Overview
School district serving Fishers and portions of southeastern Hamilton County.

## Board Structure
- **Board Members:** 7 elected members
- **Superintendent:** Appointed by board
- **Nonpartisan elections**

## Geographic Area
- Primarily serves Fishers (6th largest city in Indiana)
- Parts of Delaware, Fall Creek, and Wayne Townships

## Board Member Information
- **Term:** 4 years
- **Elections:** November of even-numbered years
- **Next Election:** November 2026
- Elected by district or at-large (varies)

## District Details
- One of the largest and fastest-growing districts in Indiana
- Rapidly expanding with Hamilton County growth
