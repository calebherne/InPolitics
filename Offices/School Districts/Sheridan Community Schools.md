# Sheridan Community Schools

## Overview
School district serving Sheridan and surrounding area in Hamilton County.

## Board Structure
- **Board Members:** Elected members (typically 5)
- **Superintendent:** Appointed by board
- **Nonpartisan elections**

## Geographic Area
- Town of Sheridan
- Parts of Adams Township

## Board Member Information
- **Term:** 4 years
- **Elections:** November of even-numbered years
- **Next Election:** November 2026
- Elected at-large or by district

## District Details
- Smallest school district in Hamilton County
- Serves town and surrounding rural area
