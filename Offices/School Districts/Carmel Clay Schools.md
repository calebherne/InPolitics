# Carmel Clay Schools

## Overview
School district serving Carmel and Clay Township in Hamilton County.

## Board Structure
- **Board Members:** 7 elected members
- **Superintendent:** Appointed by board
- **Nonpartisan elections**

## Geographic Area
- Coextensive with Clay Township
- City of Carmel

## Board Member Information
- **Term:** 4 years
- **Elections:** November of even-numbered years
- **Next Election:** November 2026
- Elected by district or at-large (varies)

## District Details
- One of Indiana's largest school districts
- Serves 5th largest city in Indiana (Carmel)
- Known for high academic performance
