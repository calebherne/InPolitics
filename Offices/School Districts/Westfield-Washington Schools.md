# Westfield-Washington Schools

## Overview
School district serving Westfield and Washington Township in Hamilton County.

## Board Structure
- **Board Members:** Elected members (typically 5-7)
- **Superintendent:** Appointed by board
- **Nonpartisan elections**

## Geographic Area
- City of Westfield
- Washington Township

## Board Member Information
- **Term:** 4 years
- **Elections:** November of even-numbered years
- **Next Election:** November 2026
- Elected by district or at-large (varies)

## District Details
- Growing rapidly with Westfield's expansion
- Newer district compared to others in county
