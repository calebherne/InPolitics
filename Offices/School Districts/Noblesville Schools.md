# Noblesville Schools

## Overview
School district serving Noblesville (county seat) and surrounding areas.

## Board Structure
- **Board Members:** Elected members (typically 5-7)
- **Superintendent:** Appointed by board
- **Nonpartisan elections**

## Geographic Area
- City of Noblesville (12th largest in Indiana)
- Noblesville Township
- Parts of White River Township

## Board Member Information
- **Term:** 4 years
- **Elections:** November of even-numbered years
- **Next Election:** November 2026
- Elected by district or at-large (varies)

## District Details
- Serves county seat
- Established district with long history
