# City Council

## Overview
Hamilton County cities use a mayor-council form of government with elected city councils serving as the legislative branch.

## Requirements
- Resident of the city
- For district seats: resident of the district
- At least 18 years old
- U.S. citizen

## Election Process
- **Size varies by city:**
  - Third-class cities: 5 members
  - Second-class cities: 7 members
  - First-class cities: 9 members (Indianapolis only)
- Members elected either:
  - By geographic districts, OR
  - At-large (city-wide)
- Most Indiana cities use districts
- Partisan elections (in most Indiana cities)

## Term
- **Length:** 4 years
- **Term Limits:** Varies by city
- **Elections:** Held in odd-numbered years (municipal elections)

## Roles
- **Part-time position** - Legislative body of city government
- Passes city ordinances
- Adopts city budget
- Sets tax rates (within state limits)
- Creates city policies
- Confirms mayoral appointments (in some cities)
- Zoning and planning decisions
- Reviews city contracts
- Holds public hearings
- Constituent services
- **Time Commitment:** Part-time, 20-30 hours/week
- **Meetings:** Weekly or bi-weekly council meetings; Committee meetings; Public hearings; Constituent meetings; Community events

## Powers
**Constitutional Powers:**
- **Legislative authority** - Passes ordinances
- **Budget approval** - Must approve city spending
- **Tax rate setting** within state limits
- **Veto override** (typically requires supermajority)
- **Zoning authority** - Land use decisions
- **Appointment confirmation** (in some cities)

**Procedural Powers:**
- Can refuse to pass mayor's proposals
- Budget amendments can redirect city priorities
- Zoning decisions can block or enable development
- Public hearings provide platform for issues
- Committee assignments control information flow
- Can investigate city departments
- Ordinances can limit mayoral authority
- Tax decisions affect residents and businesses
- Platform for higher office
- Can create or eliminate city programs through budget
- Veto override attempts can embarrass mayor
